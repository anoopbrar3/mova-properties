Who We Are

Built to build
what endures.

MOVA Properties is a private equity real estate developer, builder, and asset manager focused on design-led multifamily communities in British Columbia. We acquire, design, build, and operate — and we don't hand off what we make.

We were founded on a simple conviction: that the best buildings are made with the end in mind — not the exit. That discipline runs through every site we acquire, every material we specify, every subcontractor we trust with our work.

The result is a portfolio where design and financial performance reinforce each other. Communities residents are proud to live in. Assets investors trust to hold. Buildings that earn their place, and hold it.

Founder

Anoop Brar

Founder & Principal · MOVA Properties

Anoop Brar built MOVA around a belief that most real estate is built for the wrong audience — developers who won't own it, buyers who won't live in it. MOVA is built differently: for the long-term owner, for the resident, for the investor who thinks in decades.

His background spans development, construction management, design oversight, and private investment. He has been involved in projects ranging from custom luxury residences to CMHC-backed multifamily development — and at every scale, the standard has been the same.

Anoop runs MOVA with direct involvement at every stage of every project. Not because he has to — because the quality of the outcome depends on it. That's the model. That's the standard. And it doesn't change.

Mission

Why we exist.

To develop multifamily communities that raise the standard of what rental living can be — designed with intention, built with discipline, and operated with a long-term owner's care.

Vision

What we're building toward.

A portfolio of enduring residential assets across British Columbia — properties that appreciate not because of the market, but because they were built to last. Places where design and financial performance compound together over decades.

Values

How we work.

No compromise on material. No shortcuts on craft. Full accountability at every stage. We build what we'd want to live in — and we hold what we build. Those two commitments enforce everything else.

Our Approach

We don't build to sell.
We build to keep.

Most developers build for the exit. Materials are chosen to hit a price point, not to endure. Design is value-engineered, not valued. The building is a product — sold and forgotten the day the last unit closes.

MOVA is the opposite. We underwrite every project as a 20-year hold. That changes everything: how we choose the site, how we design the building, who we hire to build it, and how we operate it once it's complete. Long-term ownership is not a marketing position — it's the filter through which every decision gets made.
