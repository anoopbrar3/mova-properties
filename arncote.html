Design Vision · The Arncote

01

The Interior Standard

Light oak floors, two-tone cabinetry, stone counters, integrated appliances. The Arncote set the interior standard that MOVA now applies at every scale. These aren't rental-grade finishes — they're the finishes of a home worth staying in, priced for the market, built for the hold.

02

The Exterior Presence

The Arncote's facade was designed to read as a single, considered object — not a stack of units. The light rendering palette with dark window surrounds and strong horizontal lines creates a building that lifts the streetscape around it rather than blending into it.

03

Design-Led Retention

The Arncote reached 100% occupancy and held it. Not because the rents were low — because the homes were worth the rent. Design-led rental is a retention strategy, not just an aesthetic choice. The Arncote proved that thesis. Every project since has been built on it.
