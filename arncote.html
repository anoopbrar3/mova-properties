Project Overview

The building that proved the model.

The Arncote is MOVA's first completed multifamily project — 21 units across 6 storeys in Langford, BC. It was the proof of concept: that you can build design-led residential rental at a scale that makes financial sense, and hold it for the long term.

Every decision in the Arncote was made with a 20-year owner's mindset. Materials chosen for durability and character, not cost-cutting. Finishes selected to attract and retain tenants who take pride in where they live. Layouts designed to feel generous without being wasteful.

Investor Lens

The Arncote is the proof of concept. This is what MOVA delivers at scale.

Design Proof of Concept

The Arncote demonstrates that design-led multifamily can be delivered profitably, operated efficiently, and held for the long term. It set the material standard and operational model that MOVA applies to every subsequent project.

Langford Market

Langford is one of the fastest-growing municipalities in BC — a supply-constrained market with strong rental demand, high employment growth, and sustained in-migration from Greater Victoria. The fundamentals that drove the Arncote have only strengthened.

Operational Track Record

Fully stabilised since occupancy. Strong retention, zero extended vacancy, resident satisfaction that reflects the design standard. The Arncote is what we point to when investors ask whether the model works. It does.
