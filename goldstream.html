Project Overview

77 homes on Goldstream Avenue — MOVA's most ambitious project yet.

Goldstream Residences is a 77-unit, 6-storey multifamily development currently in the planning phase in Langford, BC. With two levels of underground parking and a site on Goldstream Avenue — one of Langford's most connected corridors — this project is designed to become a community anchor, not just a rental building.

The design approach carries every lesson from the Arncote and the Hockley forward: considered layouts, enduring materials, outdoor spaces worth spending time in. Goldstream Residences will be the most complete expression of what MOVA builds.

Investor Lens

Goldstream Residences is MOVA's most significant pipeline asset — and the one with the most runway.

Location & Access

Goldstream Avenue is one of Langford's primary connectors — high-visibility, walkable, and well-served by transit. The site captures both the commuter and the lifestyle renter, positioned for the broadest possible demand pool at stabilisation.

Underground Parking Premium

Two levels of underground parking is a meaningful differentiator in a market where most competing product offers at-grade or structured surface only. It allows higher unit density at grade and commands a meaningful rental premium at stabilisation.

Pipeline Opportunity

Goldstream Residences represents an early-stage investment opportunity for capital partners who want to participate from the ground up — at the point where risk-adjusted returns are most favourable. We are open to discussions with qualified investors now.
