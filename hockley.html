Project Overview

MOVA's largest project. The same standard, applied at scale.

The Hockley is a 75-unit, 6-storey multifamily residence in Langford, BC — currently under construction with a target occupancy of November 2027. CMHC MLI Select insured. Build-to-hold.

The Hockley carries the same design conviction as everything MOVA builds: considered material palette, generous proportions, layouts designed around how people actually live. But at a scale that demonstrates the model can grow without compromise.

Investor Lens

75 homes in Langford's most supply-constrained rental market. The Hockley is MOVA at scale.

CMHC MLI Select

The Hockley is fully insured under CMHC's MLI Select program — delivering competitive interest rates, a 50-year amortisation, and high loan-to-cost ratios. The capital structure is designed to maximise investor equity efficiency over the hold period.

Langford Fundamentals

Langford is BC's fastest-growing municipality — with sustained in-migration, a diversified employment base, and persistent rental supply shortfall. The Hockley adds 75 quality homes to a market that desperately needs them.

Build-to-Hold Model

The Hockley will be operated by MOVA from day one of occupancy. No premature exit. No handoff. The long-term asset value is protected by the same team that designed and built it — fully aligned with investor interests through the hold.
