Our Approach

Design is not the finish.
It is the foundation.

The best buildings are shaped by long-term ownership from day one. We think about durability, maintenance, tenant retention, operating costs, and how people actually live in the space. Design is not decoration. It is part of the asset strategy.

MOVA acquires, designs, builds, and manages. We do not simply hand off. We stay with the asset.

What We Do

Invest

We identify, underwrite, and acquire residential opportunities in growth markets — structuring each project with the right mix of investor capital, construction debt, and long-term ownership discipline.

Build

We lead design, permitting, consultant coordination, procurement, and construction oversight — with a focus on quality, cost control, and accountable execution at every stage.

Manage

We stay with the asset after completion — focusing on lease-up, operations, resident experience, NOI growth, and long-term performance. We manage what we build.

Advise

We selectively advise developers, landowners, and homeowners on feasibility, pro formas, financing strategy, design direction, and construction planning.

The MOVA Method

One platform.
Four disciplines.

Design Philosophy →
01

Acquire

We identify land and building opportunities in established BC growth markets — underwriting each site for its long-term rental yield, development potential, and neighbourhood trajectory. We are not chasing volume. We are choosing carefully.

Site Selection · Underwriting · Capital Structure

Every acquisition is stress-tested for worst-case financing conditions, extended timelines, and occupancy below market. We buy what we can hold through a cycle.

02

Develop

We lead design, permitting, and pre-development — working directly with architects, engineers, and planners to create buildings that work economically, perform materially, and belong to their communities.

Architecture · Permitting · Consultant Coordination

Design decisions get made early, when they cost nothing. MOVA's design discipline ensures that material quality, unit layouts, and amenity programming are resolved before a shovel goes in the ground.

03

Build

We manage construction with accountable oversight — coordinating procurement, draw management, schedule, and quality control. Every project is treated as an asset we will own for decades, not a liability to hand off.

Construction Management · Draw Tracking · Quality Control

The Hockley is currently in construction — Draw 3 of 12 complete, 75 suites on schedule. The same discipline that built The Arncote applies at six times the scale.

04

Operate

We do not simply hand off at occupancy. MOVA manages what it builds — focusing on lease-up, resident experience, NOI growth, and long-term asset performance. Long-term ownership is not a slogan; it is an operating model.

Asset Management · Leasing · NOI Optimisation

The Arncote has been 100% occupied since delivery. The same team that built it manages it — which means every maintenance decision reflects long-term ownership, not short-term cost minimisation.

Leadership

Anoop Brar

Founder & Principal

Anoop Brar founded MOVA Properties to bring together the disciplines that shape enduring real estate: finance, design, construction, technology, and long-term ownership.

His background spans engineering, an MBA, technology project consulting at KPMG, sustaining capital projects in oil and gas, interim CEO leadership at a public aviation technology company, and hands-on real estate development across luxury homes, small multifamily, and rental housing.

At MOVA, he is directly involved in acquisition, underwriting, capital structure, design direction, consultant coordination, construction oversight, and asset strategy. The model is simple: build the right team, align the capital, execute the details, and hold what is worth owning.
