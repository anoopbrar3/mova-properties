Our Approach

Design is not the finish.
It is the foundation.

We believe the best buildings are made with a long-term owner's mindset from the very beginning. Material choices that reduce operational cost. Spatial decisions that drive tenant retention. Architecture that appreciates because it was never fashionable — only considered.

MOVA acquires, designs, builds, and operates. We do not hand off. We hold.

What We Do

Invest

We identify, underwrite, and acquire multifamily sites in supply-constrained BC markets. Disciplined capital deployment, CMHC-backed structuring, and a hold-to-own mandate.

Build

We deliver with precision — managing design, entitlements, and construction to specification. On schedule, within budget, and to a material standard our residents and investors can feel.

Manage

Resident-first operations with data-informed decision making. We manage what we build, protecting asset value, driving retention, and compounding returns over the full hold period.

Advise

We partner with landowners, developers, and capital partners at any stage — from feasibility and pro forma to design advisory, construction oversight, and asset management.

Why It Matters

Buildings that hold their value — and earn the trust of the people in them.

We don't build to sell. We build to keep. Every material decision, every spatial proportion, every subcontractor we hire is evaluated through the lens of a 20-year owner. That discipline shows in the finished product — and in the returns.

Leadership

Anoop Brar

Founder & Principal

Anoop Brar founded MOVA Properties with a conviction that the best real estate is built with the end in mind — not the exit. With a background spanning development, construction management, and private investment, he brings an operator's discipline to every deal MOVA touches.

His approach is straightforward: acquire thoughtfully, build without compromise, and hold for the long term. The result is a portfolio where design and financial performance reinforce each other — communities that residents are proud to live in, and that investors trust over decades.
