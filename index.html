Our Conviction

"Good design is not what you add.
It's what you refuse to remove."

MOVA began with a four-plex in Calgary. It grew into three landmark multifamily buildings and a portfolio of luxury homes that set a new standard on Bear Mountain. Every project since has been shaped by the same belief: that the people who live in what we build deserve more than the minimum.


We are developers who stay. We operate what we build. We answer for what we decide.

How We Work

01

Acquire with conviction

We target infill sites in supply-constrained, high-demand markets — Langford, Greater Victoria — where demographic and regulatory tailwinds compound returns over time.

02

Lever intelligently

Our multifamily projects use CMHC MLI Select financing — the most favourable program in Canada — to maximize LTV, reduce cost of capital, and protect investor equity.

03

Build to last

We specify materials that hold value: white oak, brushed brass, large-format porcelain. Tenants stay longer in beautiful spaces — and that difference shows up on the cap rate.

04

Operate and compound

We don't flip and exit. We build long-term holds that generate stable NOI, refi, and repeat — creating compounding value for investors who think in decades.
