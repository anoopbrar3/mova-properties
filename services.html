What We Offer

Services

We work alongside developers, landowners, and investors at any stage — from early feasibility and pro forma to construction oversight and design advisory.

01

Multifamily Advisory

End-to-end development advisory for multifamily residential projects — from site identification and entitlement strategy through design management, construction, and stabilisation. We bring operational experience to every stage of the project lifecycle.

  • Site acquisition & feasibility
  • Entitlement strategy & municipal engagement
  • Design direction & architect management
  • Construction coordination & draw management
  • Stabilisation & lease-up strategy

02

Finance & Pro Forma

We build detailed financial models for multifamily development — underwriting assumptions, CMHC MLI Select structuring, capital stack optimisation, and return projections. We help capital partners and developers understand exactly what they're getting into before they commit.

  • Multifamily pro forma modelling
  • CMHC MLI Select underwriting
  • Capital stack structuring
  • Sensitivity analysis & scenario planning
  • Investor reporting & LP communication

03

Construction & Owner Rep

We serve as owner's representative on residential construction projects — managing the general contractor, monitoring draw schedules, ensuring on-budget and on-spec delivery. We protect the owner's interests from groundbreak to certificate of occupancy.

  • General contractor procurement & management
  • Budget tracking & cost control
  • Construction draw review & lender coordination
  • Schedule management & milestone tracking
  • Deficiency management & occupancy coordination

04

Luxury Home Design

For private residential clients who expect the same level of material thinking and design discipline that defines MOVA's multifamily work. We bring our design philosophy — considered, enduring, nothing superfluous — to custom homes across BC.

  • Interior design direction & specification
  • Material palette development
  • Millwork & custom cabinetry design
  • Contractor selection & management
  • Full construction oversight through completion
