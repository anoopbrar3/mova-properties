Design Vision · The Southpointe Homes

01

The Craftsman Idiom, Revisited

The Southpointe Homes borrow the warmth of the craftsman tradition — cedar accents, covered entry, pitched rooflines — and reinterpret them through a contemporary lens. Black window frames, clean massing, and a restrained material palette prevent the warmth from becoming kitsch.

02

The Interior Standard

Inside, the homes carry MOVA's signature: open-plan living, quality millwork, stone counters, and an attention to proportion that makes every room feel larger than its square footage suggests. The kitchen faces the living space. The living space faces the light. Nothing is arbitrary.

03

The Duplex as Product

The duplex format is one of the most financially efficient residential products a builder can deliver — and one of the most badly done. The Southpointe Homes prove that a duplex can have the curb appeal and interior quality of a custom single-family home, at a price point that makes sense for the neighbourhood.
