Project Overview

Where MOVA's conviction about design and holding took its first form.

The Valiant is a fourplex in Calgary, AB — MOVA's first multifamily project, completed and held. Four homes designed with the same belief that would define everything that came after: that rental housing deserves the same design attention as anything else. That tenants notice quality. That a building held for decades needs to be built for decades.

The Valiant isn't the most complex project in the portfolio. It's the most important. It's where the model was tested, refined, and proved. Every decision made here shaped how MOVA approaches the 171 units that followed.

What MOVA Learned Here

Design Drives Retention

The Valiant's tenants stayed. Not because the rents were below market — because the homes were worth staying in. Design-led rental is a retention strategy, not just an aesthetic choice.

Build for the Hold

Material decisions made with a 20-year owner's mindset perform differently than those made to hit a construction budget. The Valiant was MOVA's first proof of that thesis.

Small Projects, Big Lessons

At four units, every decision is visible. There is nowhere to hide. The discipline required to build the Valiant well is the same discipline that runs through every project since — just applied at larger scale.
